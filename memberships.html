Memberships & Pricing

Please Note

All participants who wish to join our adult classes must be at least 15 years of age - exceptions may be made upon discussion with an instructor. Membership rates are on a per-month basis. Prices are before tax.


Family Rates

Families with three or more participants pay the full rate for the first two highest-priced memberships. Additional memberships are discounted at 50%.



Memberships

Adult All adult classes and open-mats $120
Youth Youth No-Gi BJJ classes $100


Non-Membership

Drop-In One class & open mat session (Mon - Fri) $20
